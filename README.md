# 🌱 Carbon Compass

**Navigate Your Journey to a Greener Future**

Carbon Compass is a sustainability awareness platform that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, sustainability recommendations, decision support, and accessibility-first design.

Built for the Google for Developers × Hack2Skill Prompt Wars Challenge.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Many people want to live more sustainably but struggle to understand how their daily choices impact the environment.

Carbon Compass helps users:

* Understand their carbon footprint
* Discover personalized sustainability actions
* Compare environmental impacts of different choices
* Simulate future carbon savings
* Build sustainable habits through challenges and coaching

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📊 Carbon Footprint Assessment

Multi-step assessment that evaluates:

* Transportation habits
* Food consumption
* Home energy usage
* Shopping behavior

---

### 🎯 Top 3 Actions

After completing the assessment, users receive:

* Personalized recommendations
* Estimated monthly CO₂ savings
* Estimated annual CO₂ savings
* Difficulty levels
* Explanations for each recommendation

---

### 🧭 Decision Assistant

Helps users compare everyday choices such as:

* Car vs Train
* Meat-based vs Plant-based meals
* Disposable vs Reusable products

Provides sustainability-focused guidance and environmental impact comparisons.

---

### 📈 Impact Simulator

Allows users to explore how lifestyle changes affect their carbon footprint through interactive simulations and visualizations.

---

### 🤖 AI Coach Experience

Conversational sustainability guidance designed to encourage environmentally responsible decision-making.

---

### 📋 Explain My Footprint

Transforms footprint calculations into easy-to-understand insights, helping users identify their largest environmental contributors.

## ♿ Accessibility First

Carbon Compass was designed with accessibility as a core principle.

### Implemented Features

✅ High Contrast Mode

✅ Color-Blind Friendly Theme

✅ Dyslexia-Friendly Reading Mode

✅ Adjustable Font Sizes

✅ Reduced Motion Mode

✅ Keyboard Navigation Support

✅ Skip-to-Content Navigation

✅ Screen Reader Friendly Components

✅ Text-to-Speech Support

✅ Voice Input Support

✅ Read Selected Text Functionality

✅ Accessible Chart Narratives

### Accessibility Goal

Carbon Compass targets WCAG 2.1 AA compliance wherever practical.
